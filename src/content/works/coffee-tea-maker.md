Coffee and Tea Maker is a small full-stack application for keeping product and price information searchable in one place.

## Approach

The frontend uses React and TypeScript for product entry and discovery. A separate Express and MongoDB service handles products, saved orders, and search.

## Source

The [frontend repository](https://github.com/arielmalada/coffee-tea-maker-frontend) and [backend repository](https://github.com/arielmalada/coffee-tea-maker-backend) document the two application layers.
