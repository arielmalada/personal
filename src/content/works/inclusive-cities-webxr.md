The team explored how an augmented-reality exhibition could make an urban experience more inclusive. I worked across the prototype design in Figma, user research, WebXR implementation, 3D assets, and submission video production.

## Contribution

I built the mobile AR prototype with React Three Fiber and Three.js, helped shape the interaction flow, produced the supporting 3D material, and edited the final presentation.

## Outcome

The project won the 2022 Inclusive Cities Hackathon. The browser demonstration requires a WebXR-capable Android device and Chrome.
